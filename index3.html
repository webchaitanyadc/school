About Vira Medicity

We are a comprehensive healthcare facility dedicated to providing quality medical services with compassion. Our hospital features modern infrastructure spread across 7.5 acres with state-of-the-art equipment and experienced medical professionals.

  • Comprehensive Care — Wide range of medical services offering tertiary-level patient care under one roof.
  • Modern Infrastructure — Contemporary design with natural lighting throughout ICU, patient rooms, and wards.
  • Expert Doctors — Highly experienced and internationally recognized medical professionals.
  • Sustainable Healthcare — Solar panels, HEPA filters, and wastewater treatment for eco-friendly operations.
