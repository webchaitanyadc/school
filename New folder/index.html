About Maharashtra Solar Business Syndicate

We are a B2B platform dedicated to making solar procurement, tendering and project execution simpler for businesses. From verified products to tender management and training — MSBS provides end-to-end support for EPCs, installers and distributors.

  • Verified Suppliers — Manufacturer & vendor verification for quality assurance.
  • Tender Support — Documentation & submission assistance for government & private tenders.
  • Training & Finance — Training programs and finance guidance for faster deployment.
