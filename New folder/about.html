About Maharashtra Solar Business Syndicate

Accelerating solar adoption with verified products, tender support, training, and project assistance.

Empowering India’s Solar Revolution

B2B Model – One Business at a Time
At Maharashtra Solar Business Syndicate, we drive the future of clean energy through a robust B2B platform dedicated exclusively to solar products and solutions. We bridge the gap between quality solar manufacturers and businesses seeking reliable, scalable, and sustainable energy solutions.

Our Vision

To become India’s most trusted B2B solar marketplace, accelerating the adoption of renewable energy through innovation, transparency, and collaboration.

Our Mission

Revolutionize the way solar products are sourced and sold by building a trusted, efficient, and technology-driven B2B marketplace.

  • Empower EPC contractors, installers, resellers, and project developers.
  • Provide seamless access to verified suppliers and competitive pricing.
  • Offer a curated marketplace of high-quality solar products.

Core Services

Finance: Assistance with funding & payment management.

Supply Chain Management: Streamlined sourcing and logistics.

Tender Management: Documentation & bidding support.

Online Training: Skill-building for EPCs and installers.

Online MSEDCL / Bank Work Support: Hassle-free administrative assistance.

B2B Sales Process

  1. Visit to client with a transparent buying process.
  2. B2B billing per order for accuracy.
  3. Online/offline form filling for data-driven deals.
  4. Branding client office/shop for visibility.
  5. Promoting only certified & reliable manufacturers and distributors.
  6. Finalize orders & transfer payments smoothly.
  7. Support product development for industrial, schools, hospitals & MW tenders.
