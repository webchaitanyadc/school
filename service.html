Insurance & Government Schemes

We provide both private and government insurance facilities to ensure stress-free access to quality medical care. Cashless treatment options are available for all insured patients, making healthcare more convenient and affordable.

Additionally, we are proud to offer treatments under the Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana (MJPJAY) and Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY) schemes, enabling free healthcare services for eligible beneficiaries. These initiatives aim to bring advanced medical care to everyone, ensuring that financial constraints never become a barrier to health and wellness.
