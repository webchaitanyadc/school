Featured Departments

ENT Department

Ear, Nose & Throat Specialist Care

Overview

Ear, nose, and throat (ENT) specialists are also known as otolaryngologists. This department deals with medical issues related to balance and hearing, sleeping and breathing issues, speech and swallowing, neck and head cancer, sinuses and allergies, skin disorders, and facial plastic surgery.

The ENT department of Vira Hospital is highly equipped with facilities to provide treatments for issues related to the ear, nose, and throat. We have a team of highly trained doctors and experienced staff who take care of the patients.

  • Endoscopy and advanced surgical procedures
  • Ophthalmology and plastic surgeries for hearing-related issues
  • Reconstruction and surgical management of benign tumors
  • Cancer treatment of the neck and head
  • Surgery of the neck and face

Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

Critical Care for Severely Ill Patients

Overview

Our highly trained critical care intensivists treat this department's most severely ill and injured persons. Vira Hospital has specialists who care for patients undergoing major trauma or surgery.

The doctors of Vira Hospital look after the people who require advanced care and monitoring. The full spectrum of this department is provided by specialists and other medical experts specially trained in critical healthcare and are there at the Hospital 24×7 to serve patients.

  • 24×7 critical care specialists
  • Advanced monitoring systems
  • Post-operative care for major surgeries
  • Trauma patient management

Dentistry Department

Comprehensive Dental Care

Overview

The Dentistry Department at Vira Hospital treats both children's and adults' dental issues. The professionals examine the patient's needs and condition to provide multidisciplinary care.

We have a team of licensed dental specialists who provide routine care and special practices to ensure treatment for every dental problem. In addition, our specialists help the patient manage issues relating to dental hygiene, dental oncology, dental sleep medicine, etc.

  • Comprehensive dental examinations
  • Routine dental care and preventive treatments
  • Dental oncology services
  • Dental sleep medicine
  • Pediatric and adult dental care

General Surgery Department

Expert Surgical Care

Overview

Vira Hospital is one of the most experienced hospitals in general surgery practice. We have a team of highly-trained professionals who have conducted over 2000 laparoscopic surgeries in a year.

Our surgeons are the best surgeons you can find in the country as they know whether you need surgery or the disease can be cured through medicines or physiotherapy. Plus, we are available at your service 24×7 for emergencies and other trauma-related services.

  • Over 2000 laparoscopic surgeries annually
  • 24×7 emergency and trauma services
  • Diagnostic, preventive, and therapeutic procedures
  • Minimally invasive surgical techniques
  • Comprehensive pre and post-operative care

Ophthalmology Department

Advanced Eye Care Services

Overview

Vira Hospital contains the most modern equipment and facility to look into conditions relating to eye problems. It can be any problem relating to glaucoma, cornea, pediatric ophthalmology, Vitro retina, and oculoplastic.

Our highly trained doctors look into different symptoms of eye infection, cataracts, nerve problems and other eye diseases. We are one of the major eye chains of India that work forefront in the field of ophthalmic care using advanced medical techniques.

  • Glaucoma diagnosis and treatment
  • Corneal disease management
  • Pediatric ophthalmology services
  • Vitro retina treatments
  • Oculoplastic surgery
  • Cataract surgery with advanced techniques
